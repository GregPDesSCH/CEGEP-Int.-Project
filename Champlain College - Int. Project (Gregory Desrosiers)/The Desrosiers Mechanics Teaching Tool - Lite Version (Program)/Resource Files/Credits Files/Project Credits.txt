Thank you for using The Desrosiers Mechanics Teaching Tool - Lite Version.



This is the graduation project of me, Gregory Desrosiers, all the way from
Champlain College Saint-Lambert in Montreal, Canada.

We at Champlain College have worked so hard to exercise our own knowledge of
computer science and mathematics by expanding our knowledge of Oracle's
JAVA programming language over three semesters, and then using both this
and the material we've learned from our program, excel our intelligence with
the development of our integrative projects.

As for me, it's been a hard-working effort in developing the project. I did
seek into developing a lot more than implemented realistically, but because
of the reality of semesters with several other courses to worry about, I
have decided to put a lite version of my full project.

Nevertheless, I hope this project turns out the best for you as we students
tackle new topics and horizons into the future. You'll never know whether
a project will turn into excellence, so keep an eye out!

So this is me, signing off. And I hope future knowledge turns out best for
me and all the people who may reach out my projects, including you.
